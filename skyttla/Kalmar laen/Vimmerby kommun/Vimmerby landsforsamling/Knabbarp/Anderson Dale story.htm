Dale Anderson, Scott City
Born on a farm near Ragan, Nebr. Then moved to a farm north of Holdrege. Dale went to school in Holdrege and graduated from high school in 1934. In the fall 0f ´34, Dale went to work for what recently was known as the J.M.McDonald Co and worked for the total of 34 years of which 38 years were spent in managing stores in Wayne, Nebr., Albion, Nebr., and Scott City, KS. Dale and Alice moved to Scott City in May of 1960 to manage the Scott City store. Alice passed away in March 1978. Dale retired from McDonalds Febr 1, 1981.

Ruth Anne was born to Mr. & Mrs. Bruce Allen in Ellsworth, KS. 18 months later she moved with her parents to Winona, KS. Dale and Ruth Anne are retired and spend a lot of time in community service and church work. They belong to Holy Cross Lutheran Church.

[Skrivet juni 1988]
